Two Sides

by Yeqian Li

Research

My inspiration comes from Shanghai "socialite". This is a group of young girls who looks smart but rents expensive things together with their living salary, just to attract men to spend money for them. Beneath the luxury is the life at the subsistence level. In this project, I want to show more "two sides" of human nature. Therefore, I select six characters with strong contrast show what do they look like and their true features through game book.

Sketch

I looked for six people whose appearance is very different from the real situation. These people look like the ordinary people around us, and each character represented a typical group.
